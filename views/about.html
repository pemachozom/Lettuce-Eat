About Us

ITeria a small café based here in Gyalposhing College of Information Technology. “Think authentic Bhutan non veg food, and chances are this is where you will land up. Try the set meals starting with hot beverages like hot coffee, going on to serving of snacks like pakora, fried-rice etc.”

Our Mission

Students and staffs gets busy with their work, so while ordering food during their break times they tend to wait in queue for their orders to arrive which takes times most of the times. In order to avoid that we came up with this website so we don't have to face those.
